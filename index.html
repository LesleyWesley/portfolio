Who am I, anyway?

I'm a huge comic book nerd, and was originally inspired to learn to code websites from reading Batgirl comics. I quickly fell in love with the process of building websites from the ground up, and used my time in quarantine to level up my skills.

Before making the switch to a tech career, I managed a movie theater for 6 years. Before that, I attended the University of Missouri, where I studied English Literature, History and Art History.

When I'm not working on new web projects, I love playing video games, watching movies, and relaxing with my favorite music.
