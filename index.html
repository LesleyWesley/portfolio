Latest Projects

Dorothy DeLong Portfolio
Designed in Adobe XD
Built with HTML5, CSS3, & JavaScript

A one-page portfolio for feminist photographer Dorothy DeLong. This design is purposely minimalist to allow the client's photography to shine. The black and white color scheme, along with the typewriter-style typography, highlight the raw, candid nature of the client's photography, while the neon purple photo overlays and other details add a touch of excitement and femininity.

Sit Conmigo Website
Designed in Adobe XD
Built with HTML5, CSS3, & JavaScript

A fully-responsive, one-page website for an environmentally-friendly chair company. For this design, I went for a Miami-inspired Art Deco aesthetic to highlight the tropical vibe of the company and products. In order to mirror the environmental focus of the company, I opted for an earthy color scheme of greens and tans.

I Should Probably Introduce Myself...

I'm a huge comic book nerd, and was originally inspired to learn to design and code websites from reading Batgirl comics. I quickly fell in love with the process of designing and building websites from the ground up, and used my time in quarantine to level up my skills.

Before making the switch to a tech career, I managed a movie theater for 6 years, and before that, I studied English Literature, History, and Art History at the University of Missouri. When I'm not working on new web projects, I love playing video games, studying Korean, watching movies, and relaxing with my favorite music.
